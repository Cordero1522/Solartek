How Solartek Works

Capture Attic Heat

Our system efficiently captures excess heat that builds up in your attic space, even during winter months when outdoor temperatures are low.

Heat Transfer

A high-efficiency heat exchanger transfers the captured thermal energy from your attic to your pool water.

Warm Pool Water

The heated water returns to your pool, raising and maintaining a comfortable temperature for swimming regardless of outdoor conditions.

Product Specifications

Performance & Capacity

  • Pool Sizes: Up to 1000 sq ft or 35,000 gallons
  • Nominal BTU Rating: 60,000 BTUs/hour @ Δ32°F
  • BTU Transfer Range: 20,000-150,000 BTU's per hour
  • Operating Cost: Less than $15 per month
  • Example: Pool water input: 72°F & Attic's Peak @ 104°F

Physical Specifications

  • Size: 27"H x 17"D x 47"W
  • Weight: 136 lbs (Boxed: 145 lbs)
  • Power: 115 VAC, 3.6 AMPS @ Full Load (405 watts max)
  • Noise Level: Very quiet - 62 db A-weighted, 67 db C-weighted @10 feet
  • Attic Space Required: 3 ft min height to peak and square feet of attic floor equal to or greater than pool surface area
