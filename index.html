Our Attic Installation System

A compact and highly efficient unit designed for discreet and effortless installation in your home’s attic. It optimizes energy use, achieving reductions of 20 – 30%, while easing the workload on your air conditioning system—helping significantly extend its overall lifespan.

Technical Specifications

System Details

  • Pool Sizes: Up to 1000 square feet or 35,000 gallons
  • BTU Rating: 60,000 BTU's/hour @ Δ32°F
  • Example: Pool water at 72°F and Attic at 104°F
  • Transfer Range: 20,000-150,000 BTU's per hour
  • Dimensions: 27" Height x 17" Depth x 47" Width
  • Weight: 136 lbs (145 lbs packaged)
  • Power: 115 VAC 3.6 AMPS at full load (405 watts max)

Requirements and Features

  • Required Attic Space: Minimum height of 3 feet to the peak and square feet of attic floor equal to or greater than the pool surface area
  • Noise Level: Very quiet: 62 db (A-weighted) 67 db (C-weighted) @10 feet
  • Operating Cost: Less than $15 per month
  • Freeze Protection: Optional automatic drain-down
  • Manufacturing: Made in USA
  • Patents: US patents issued
